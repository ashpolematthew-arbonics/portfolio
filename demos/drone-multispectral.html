🛰️ Multispectral drone restoration planning real data

My own DJI Mavic 3 Multispectral flights (G / R / red-edge / NIR + RGB) over degraded savanna near Bolgatanga, northern Ghana. Each frame is band-registered, turned into real NDVI & NDRE, classified, and every major tree crown delineated — then each patch of ground is matched to the right restoration intervention: Agroforestry on cultivated land, Reforestation on bare degraded ground, and Assisted Natural Regeneration where shrubs are already coming back. Switch scenes and layers; it all runs in your browser.

How this works — data & method (with literature)

Sensor. DJI Mavic 3M, ~5.7 cm GSD. The four MS lenses (G, R, RE, NIR) have parallax offsets, co-registered to red by high-pass phase cross-correlation; the wider-FOV 20 MP RGB is fitted to the MS grid by ORB features + RANSAC similarity.

Indices. NDVI (Rouse 1974) and red-edge NDRE (Gitelson & Merzlyak 1994). Tree crowns: smoothed-NDVI local maxima (Popescu & Wynne 2004) + marker-controlled watershed (Ke & Quackenbush 2011).

Matching land to intervention. Rather than a single "eligible" mask, each patch is matched to the restoration approach that fits its condition — the way NbS programmes actually plan:

  • Agroforestry — open ground with a high tillage signature (cultivated). Trees are integrated into the farming system; cultivation is a positive, not an exclusion.
  • Reforestation — bare, minimally-vegetated degraded ground away from cultivation. Direct tree planting.
  • Assisted Natural Regeneration (ANR) — shrub / woody regrowth with few mature trees. Protect and manage what is already returning.
  • Existing canopy — delineated mature crowns are conserved, not planted (the forest test).

The tillage layer is a gradient-orientation coherence index (ploughed furrows are unidirectional); the slider sets the agroforestry/reforestation split. Each intervention carries its own indicative sequestration rate for a first-order ex-ante estimate.

Honest limitations. Single dry-season frames with no calibration panel: indices are relative, and cropland vs bare can't be split spectrally (hence the tillage-texture proxy). Altitude wasn't logged, so area is estimated at ~100 m AGL.
