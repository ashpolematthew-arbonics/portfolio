How this works — the method behind the demo

Each parcel carries attributes a real screening pipeline derives from geospatial layers: land-cover time series (Sentinel-2 / Dynamic World for the 10-year forest test), a DEM-derived slope raster, peat & wetland maps, protected-area boundaries (WDPA), and prior-use classification.

A parcel is eligible only if it passes every enabled rule — a logical AND across criteria. Disabling a rule relaxes the screen, which is why the eligible area grows as you uncheck boxes. The eligible-area sum drives a first-order credit estimate at a nominal 8 tCO₂e/ha/yr.

In production this same logic runs over thousands of parcels from cadastral data, with drone orthomosaics used to ground-truth borderline cases.
