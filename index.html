About

I'm a data scientist working at the intersection of forestry, remote sensing and carbon-market science. My work spans the full measurement, reporting and verification (MRV) chain: building species-specific growth models calibrated to field inventory, deriving forest structure from drone and airborne LiDAR, designing statistically-defensible sampling schemes for Verra/VCS projects, and shipping the tooling that lets project developers screen land at scale.

These demos are self-contained reconstructions of that work using synthetic data, so nothing here exposes client or proprietary information — but the methods, formulas and structure are the real thing.
